Privacy Policy

For the chickvideogames.com website · Last updated: 7 July 2026

This policy covers the Chick Video Games website only. It is a simple, static site — it does not use cookies, analytics or tracking of any kind, and it does not collect personal information from visitors.

The short version. Browsing this site is anonymous to us. There are no cookies, no analytics, no advertising and no trackers. The only time we receive any information is if you choose to email us — and then we only see what you send.

Who we are

This website is operated by Chick Video Games, an independent game studio. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at contact@chickvideogames.com.

Information this website collects

None automatically. This site is a set of static pages. It sets no cookies, runs no analytics or advertising scripts, and does not fingerprint or profile you. We do not build any record of who visits.

As with any website, our hosting and network providers may process the technical request data inherent to serving a page (such as your IP address) transiently to deliver the site and protect it from abuse. We do not have access to that data as an identifiable profile, and we do not use it to track you.

If you email us

The contact link on this site opens your own email app addressed to contact@chickvideogames.com. There is no form on the site and nothing is submitted through your browser. If you write to us, we receive the email you send and use it only to reply to you. We do not add you to any mailing list.

Our games have their own policies

This policy is about the website. Each of our games is a separate product with its own privacy policy describing what that game does — for example age handling, cloud saves, leaderboards and advertising. You can always find a game's policy from its store listing and from within the game. For our current title, Cozy Block Garden, the policy is at cbg.chickvideogames.com/privacy-policy.html.

Children's privacy

This website is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. Age handling and children's-privacy protections that apply to a specific game are described in that game's own privacy policy.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above.
